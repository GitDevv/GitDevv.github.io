The Story Of Mobile Luggage Storage

We have all, at one time or another, spent half a day travelling from the airport to our hotel only to transport and store our luggage. We too, have lugged our luggage across cobblestone, busy public transit and sidewalks.

WE HAVE A SOLUTION...

01 Same-day luggage Pick-up & Delivery

Discover BagsAway, a Toronto based Mobile luggage storage company! We'll pick-up your luggage anywhere in Toronto's downtown core, securely store your luggage and drop off your luggage at any convinient location in Toronto.

We service Toronto’s Pearson Airport, Toronto’s Billy Bishop Airport, Greater Toronto Area hotels, offices and residences.

We are very busy this time of year so we require the delivery time be no sooner than 4 hours after luggage pick-up. For example, if you would like your luggage picked up at Person airport at 10am, we can drop your luggage back to you at your hotel at 2pm or later.

02 Safe, Secure, Convenient Luggage Storage

We are the most secure bag storage services in Toronto. The safety of your luggage is our primary concern. When our team picks up luggage from you, we secure each bag with tie wraps to guarantee the luggage is not opened or interfered with.

Our transport vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking and CCTV camera’s to allow multiple eyes to be on your luggage at all times.

All BagsAway employees are fully qualified to execute their function with safety and security of luggage being their main concern.

We provide secure bag storages with your information also being safe with us. BagsAway does not distribute or share any customer personal information. The information we ask for is the basic information we need in order to arrange for a safe and trustworthy service.

03Overnight Luggage Storage

Our same day mobile luggage storage service is something unique to the world and is also the perfect one in terms of customer service.

Our system automatically tracks your flight status and any delays will automatically be integrated into your pick-up time. A member of our team will be expecting your arrival at the airport and will have a sign with your name on it, at the exit of the airport baggage pick-up.
