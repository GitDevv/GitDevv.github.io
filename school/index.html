Parkholme is a school in the Peel District School Board for students 14 to 21 years of age with special needs.

All students have developmental disabilities and in addition many students have a diagnosis of autism or other medical complications. We are located in the city of Brampton.

Toward Independence is the goal for all of our students. We focus on supporting students to develop communication skills, functional academics and life skills.
